Description

  • A semantic model combining a SKOS taxonomy of design uncertainty and mitigation concepts with additional classes and properties for phase, stakeholder, nature, object, and action.

  • A combined ontology and SKOS vocabulary for representing design uncertainty and mitigation strategies in building projects.

  • The SKOS concept scheme used within the Design Uncertainty Model.

  • A concept scheme describing types of design uncertainty and mitigation strategies in building projects.
